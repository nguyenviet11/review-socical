Media Mister Review: The Unbiased Truth

  • Special Contributor
  • July 15, 2022

Is Media Mister the right place to buy social media engagements? Read this Media Mister review to determine if they live up to the hype.

Media Mister Review: The Unbiased Truth

Media Mister is one of the oldest and best known social media growth companies in business today. If you are looking to grow your following on social media, you may be thinking about paying for engagement from a company like Media Mister, but you are likely hesitant to move forward with the purchase until you know if this is a legit strategy. I had the same concerns, so I decided to give it a try and evaluate the results. On this page, you will learn everything you need to know about Media Mister and the services they provide. 

Pros and Cons of Media Mister

At first glance, Media Mister really seems to have a lot to offer. I was a little worried that their services seemed to good to be true, but after some additional research, I decided to push forward with my test to see how things went. Overall, it was a very positive experience with just a few things that I might change. 

Pros of Media Mister: 

  • Surprisingly Affordable While Media Mister is not the cheapest option in the industry, they are surprisingly affordable for the quality results. 
  • High Quality Followers The followers that are provided from Media Mister are real users so they wont disappear over time. 
  • Lots of Social Media Options While I only tested their services on TikTok so far, they also offer likes, follows, comments, and other engagement across virtually every social media platform available. 
  • Experienced Company This is one of the oldest and most established social media marketing companies around so they really know what they are doing. 
  • Constantly Updated There are always new social media platforms coming into existence and becoming popular. Media Mister keeps a close eye on the environment and adds in new services as needed to ensure you always have access to the social media engagements you need.

Cons of Media Mister: 

  • Can Take Time Depending on how many social media engagements you purchase, it can take several days for them to be completed. This is because they are offering high quality engagements that take time to complete. 
  • No Free Trial Media Mister does not offer a free trial. They do have very affordable entry level packages though so you can try them out with minimal investment. 

 

TL;DR

Media Mister is one of the best-known social media marketing companies in the industry, and they have earned an excellent reputation. I used their services to purchase some followers on TikTok and I am thrilled with the results. No matter what your social media goals, Media Mister can help you accomplish them. 

What is Media Mister?

Media Misterr is a social media marketing and engagement company. This means that they can help you to get more followers and interactions on your social media posts. By paying for this engagement, your social media accounts will grow much faster, which can translate into greater success. 

The algorithms that are in place on social media platforms will see that your accounts are growing quickly, which will then prompt them to show up in the search results for other users on the sites. This can help to spur on greater organic growth. In addition, the likes, comments, follows, and other engagement will serve as social proof that you are successful, which can help to prompt more people to follow you. 

Who Needs Media Mister Services?

Anyone who wants to build up a following on social media can benefit from Media Misters services. This would include all businesses (including small businesses), social influencers, bloggers, musicians, artists, and even just individuals who want to be active on social media. 

Media Mister offers a variety of different service options so whether you are a huge corporation or a small local business, they have what you need. They can help you build up your following on a single social media platform or across multiple different platforms based on your social media marketing strategy and budget. Media Mister can also be used successfully whether you are already established on social media or you are just starting out and you want to trigger activity by buying social media signals. 

What are the Types of Engagements Available from Media Mister?

One of my favorite things about the services that are offered by Media Mister is that they have so many different options. Each social media platform is going to have a different set of engagement types that are possible based on the options for that site. 

For example, if you want to buy TikTok engagements like I did, you can choose from buying followers, comments, likes, shares, or views. If you are looking to build your YouTube channel, on the other hand, you will have options to buy views, likes, subscribers, favorites, shares, watch time hours, dislikes, and comments. 

Media Mister Features:

  • Real Engagements I received real engagements from real people that had other natural activity on them, which is exactly what I was hoping for. 
  • Natural Delivery Time After I ordered the TikTok engagements, they began flowing onto my account right away. They continued to trickle in over the course of the next couple days so that it appeared very natural. 
  • Wide Range of Options While I only purchased TikTok engagements, I was impressed with how many different options they have available. If I ever want to launch a diverse social media marketing campaign, this will definitely be where I come. 
  • Targeted Services I love that I could purchase targeted services. This means that my engagement was coming from accounts in the geographic region that I am targeting! 
  • Refund Guarantee While there is no free trial, they do offer a refund guarantee if you do not get the total number of likes (or other engagements) that you paid for within the promised delivery time.  
  • Multiple Payment Options Paying for these services was quick and easy. I liked that I could use a number of different payment methods based on my preference. 
  • Privacy Whenever buying social media engagements, you want to keep it private. All my information is kept private so I dont have to worry about social media platforms finding out. 
  • Customer Support The customer support team was very responsive and friendly.  

How Safe are Media Mister Engagements? 

One of the things that I liked most about Media Mister is that they guarantee their engagements are of the highest quality. This means that you will be getting likes, follows, comments, and other engagements from real users instead of just fake bot accounts like many other companies provide. 

Not only did this help to ensure my account was not at risk, but it also gave me confidence that the engagements that I paid for would remain in place long into the future. It has been several weeks since I made the purchase for my TikTok account and I am still enjoying the benefits of the increased engagements. 

Pricing of Media Mister Services

After browsing through the different price points for some of the most popular social media platforms, I was happy with what I saw. Given the fact that I am buying real user engagement with a guarantee, these prices are very reasonable. Each social media platform will have its own specific prices based on what type of engagement you are purchasing. Some of the key price points for the biggest social media sites are as follows: 

  • Instagram Growth You can buy 50 Instagram followers for as little as $2 or up to 5000 followers for under $95, depending on where in the world you want them to be from. 
  • TikTok Growth 25 TikTok followers will run you $2 if you get them from anywhere in the world. 5000 followers will be just $189. 
  • Clubhouse Growth You can get 100 clubhouse followers for just $13. 
  • Facebook Growth Pick up 100 Facebook profile followers for just $3. Note that with Facebook you can choose not just where the followers are from, but also things like their gender and other specific attributes. Choosing specific profile attributes will impact the price. 
  • YouTube Growth 50 YouTube subscribers will cost you just $10. 

What About the Media Mister Customer Support Team?

Personally I never ran into any issues that would have caused me to have to reach out to the customer support team for help. I do, however, recognize that customer support is critical for this type of service, so I contacted them anyway. I was pleasantly surprised at how quickly they replied to my live chat request. I went ahead and asked them a few questions about how their services worked and how to purchase multiple different things at once. They gave me clear and easy way to follow answers. 

Media Mister Alternatives

While I definitely like Media Mister and will be using them again in the future, it is always good to have some alternative options as well. In my research, I found that another popular option is a service called GetAFollower, which offers similar high-quality social media engagement solutions.

Final Verdict

After spending quite a bit of time browsing through the Media Mister site and making a purchase to promote my TikTok account, I have no doubt that this is one of the best options available for building my social media accounts. I love how easy it is to select what type of engagements you need, and how they have the option to specify what part of the world the engagements will come from. 

Also, the fact that this is such an affordable service makes it a great option for promoting any new (or established) social media account I have. If you are thinking about purchasing social media engagements, I definitely recommend Media Mister.
